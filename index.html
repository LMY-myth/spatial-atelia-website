Why We Dare to Offer "Unlimited"

Many ask us: "How can you offer unlimited revisions and renderings without going bankrupt?" The answer is simple: We get it right the first time.

With 16 years of on-site execution experience in Shanghai, we don't just "guess" your style; we understand the engineering logic behind the beauty. Our mature communication system ensures we grasp your vision instantly, minimizing the back-and-forth that plagues junior designers.

We don't offer "Unlimited" because we expect you to need it forever. We offer it to give you absolute peace of mind.

Brand Story & Promise

We are a dedicated design team hailing from Shanghai, China. Over the past 16 years, situated in this city where tradition meets modernity, we have accumulated extensive experience in local architecture and interior design. Unlike purely conceptual design, our 16 years of hands-on practice have given us an almost instinctual and profound understanding of "how to build." We understand that excellent design should not remain solely on paper; it must be fully realizable. We bring this rigorous standard of craftsmanship and structural integrity from our offline work to the online world, ensuring that every remotely delivered project meets the same professional precision as our local endeavors.

In recent years, as more design commissions from overseas have reached our desks, we have heard the call of the global market. In 2026, we are officially expanding globally, establishing new connections through Pinterest, Instagram, and Fiverr. We aim not merely to provide a service, but to inject a fresh source of energy and perspective from the East into the global interior design landscape.

Our Promise: In an era where AI is prevalent, we stand firm on 100% human-crafted design and drafting. We ensure that every detail embodies the thought, care, and "human touch" of a real designer. Backed by the scale of our mature team, we promise to provide rapid delivery, unlimited renderings, and unlimited revisions until the ideal space you envision is perfectly realized.

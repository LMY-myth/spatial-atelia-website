Workflow

1

Modeling Stage

This is the initial stage where you suggest changes and we will plan the space according to your needs and intended style. This includes the functional layout and style choices, and we will proceed to the next step after we have received your approval of the model.

2

Low Resolution Rendering Stage

This is a key stage for you to suggest changes and we will continue to modify the project based on your comments until we have your approval. We will show you alower resolution rendering of the finished project, where you can make specific adjustments to all the design elements such as layout, shape, materials election, and color scheme.

3

High Resolution Rendering Stage

This is the final stage of our work, after all the details have been confirmed, we will carryout the real rendering, which usually takes a few hours(90 minutes or so for a single sheet). Of course, if you find that you have missed a design detail, don’t worry, you are always welcome, you can always ask and we will correct it (this may result in a longer working time)
